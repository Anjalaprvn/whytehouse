Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our packages, bookings, and services.

Bookings & Payments

You can book a package by browsing our Packages page and submitting an enquiry form, or by contacting us directly via phone or WhatsApp. Our team will confirm availability and guide you through the booking process.
We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, and other standard payment methods. Full payment details will be shared upon booking confirmation.
Yes, a booking is confirmed only after receipt of the required advance payment. The advance amount varies by package and will be communicated by our team at the time of booking.
Yes, once your booking is confirmed and payment is received, you will receive a confirmation with your itinerary details via email or WhatsApp.

Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email. Refund eligibility depends on how far in advance the cancellation is made. Certain peak-season bookings may be non-refundable, which will be clearly stated at the time of booking.
Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days to the original payment method.
Yes, rescheduling is possible subject to availability. Please contact us as early as possible and we will do our best to accommodate your new dates.

Packages & Itineraries

Absolutely. We offer fully customisable itineraries tailored to your preferences, budget, and travel dates. Reach out to our team and we'll put together a personalised plan for you.
Meal inclusions vary by package. Each package listing clearly states what is included. If you have specific dietary requirements, please let us know at the time of booking.
Some packages include flights while others are land-only. This is clearly mentioned in each package description. We can also assist with flight bookings upon request.
Most of our packages are available for individuals, couples, families, and groups. There is no strict minimum, though pricing may vary based on group size. Contact us for group-specific quotes.

Travel Documents & Visas

We provide guidance on visa requirements for international destinations and can assist with documentation. However, the responsibility for obtaining a valid visa rests with the traveller.
For international travel, you will need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), the required visa, and any destination-specific permits. For domestic travel, a government-issued photo ID is sufficient.

Hospitality & Properties

We manage a range of properties including villas, resorts, and holiday homes across various destinations. Each property is carefully selected to ensure quality and comfort.
You can submit an enquiry directly from the property's detail page on our website, or contact us via phone or WhatsApp and our team will assist you.
